The purpose of financial reporting is to provide information about the company's performance. Who will use such information? 

- Investors in **debt securities**: the ability of company to pay interest & repay the principal lent.
- Investors in **equity securities**: the ability of company to pay dividends & future increasing of share price.

## Scope of Financial Statements Analysis

Financial reporting support the economical decision making. Decision maker make decision with the exception of company's future performance which depended on the current & past performance. Such exception includes **profitability**<sup> [1]</sup> and **ability  to generate positive cash flow**<sup> [2]</sup>.
